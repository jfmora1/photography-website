About Me

Hi, I’m Jay! For over 10 years I’ve been telling stories through photography, and in the last couple of years I’ve fallen in love with videography too. From working as a DP on short films to helping small businesses create videos and photos for their websites and social media, I’ve found so much joy in bringing ideas to life.

I’ve been lucky to work with amazing people - agencies, brands, and local creatives — and I always aim to make the process as fun and collaborative as possible. Whether it’s a big project or something more personal, I want everyone I work with to feel comfortable and excited about what we’re creating together.

At the end of the day, I see both photography and video as more than just my job—they’re my way of connecting with people and sharing stories that matter.
